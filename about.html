ABOUT BHARAT CRICKET CLUB

Welcome to Bharat Cricket Club, Dallas-Fort Worth's premier cricket team! Our mission is to foster a love for cricket and promote teamwork and excellence in the sport. We are proud to participate in three premier leagues: DFCL, LECA, and LECA FieldsManager.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to building a strong cricketing community and achieving excellence both on and off the field.

With over 70 dedicated players across two championship teams - Bharat Warriors and Bharat Yodhas - we embody the spirit of teamwork, passion, and competitive excellence. Our community-driven approach ensures that every player, from beginners to seasoned professionals, finds a home where they can grow and excel.
