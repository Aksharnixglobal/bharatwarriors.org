Official Announcement ‚Äî Expansion of Bharat Cricket Club By UGNT

September 2025

We are excited to share a new chapter in the journey of Bharat Cricket Club By UGNT.

Starting with the DFCL Fall Cup 2025, our club will proudly field two teams:

Bharat Warriors
+
Bharat Yodhas

This expansion comes in response to the growing interest, dedication, and availability of players within our club. By registering two teams, we aim to provide more opportunities, more game time, and a stronger platform for every player to showcase their skills and passion for cricket.

At Bharat Cricket Club , our mission has always been to build a community rooted in teamwork, sportsmanship, and excellence on and off the field. With this step forward, we are confident of bringing double the energy, double the fight, and double the spirit to DFCL 2025.

We thank our players, supporters, and sponsors for their continuous encouragement and look forward to an exciting season ahead.
